BroBlock scans tweets for hustle culture, fake gurus, engagement bait, and crypto grift — then frosts them so you can scroll in peace.

How It Works

1
Scan
Every tweet is analyzed as it appears in your feed.
2
Score
Pattern matching across 16 categories produces a Bro Score (0-100).
3
Frost
High scorers get blurred. You decide who stays and who goes.
